Seasonal Recipes

Heirloom Tomato Salad

Ingredients: 2 lbs heirloom tomatoes, 1/4 cup fresh basil, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Slice tomatoes and arrange on a platter. Tear basil leaves and scatter over tomatoes. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve fresh.

Zucchini Noodles with Pesto

Ingredients: 2 large zucchini, 1/2 cup homemade or store-bought pesto, 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, 2 tbsp parmesan cheese.

Instructions: Spiralize zucchini into noodles. Sauté noodles in a pan over medium heat for 2-3 minutes. Toss with pesto and halved cherry tomatoes. Sprinkle with parmesan before serving.

Kale and Sweet Potato Bowl

Ingredients: 1 bunch kale, 1 large sweet potato, 1 can chickpeas, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika, lemon juice.

Instructions: Dice sweet potato and roast with olive oil and paprika at 400°F for 25 minutes. Sauté kale with drained chickpeas until wilted. Combine in a bowl, drizzle with lemon juice, and serve warm.

Roasted Root Vegetable Medley

Ingredients: 2 carrots, 2 parsnips, 1 beet, 1 red onion, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp rosemary, salt, pepper.

Instructions: Chop vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Toss with olive oil, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Roast at 425°F for 35-40 minutes, stirring halfway. Serve as a side or over quinoa.
